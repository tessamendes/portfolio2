Minimalife

The Minimalife (Minimal Life) app aims to help the user reduce the number of material items one owns and buys in order to help achieve financial freedom and peace of mind.

This app uses positive reinforcement, gamification and statistical habit tracking to motivate users with their past success, and encourage them when they are struggling.

Yelp Usability

Yelp guerilla-style usability test to identify issues with ordering take-out or delivery from restaurants.

There were two major questions I sought out to answer: Can users easily search for places that offer take-out or delivery? Can users save a place for later quickly if they are looking at multiple menus?
